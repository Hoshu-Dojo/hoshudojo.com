Full Schedule

  1. Saturday, March 9th
    1. Registration - 9:00 am to 9:30 am
    2. Morning Seminar - 9:30 am to 12:00 noon
    3. Lunch - 12 noon to 1:00 pm
    4. Afternoon Seminar - 1:00 pm to 2:00 pm
    5. Grading - 2:00 pm to 4:00 pm
    6. Dinner - 5 pm to 5:45 pm Location TBD
  2. Sunday, March 10th
    1. Registration - 9:00 am to 9:30 am

The exact times may change as we get closer to the event. We will send updates when things change, but please check here for updates!

Location

École Qayqayt Elementary: 85 Merivale St, New Westminster, BC V3L 0G2.

Seminar

Early bird prices: fees paid up to February 24th will cost as follows:

  • Saturday: $80 per person
  • Sunday: $40 per person

Fees paid after February 24th will cost the same as above + $20.

Lunch

Lunch will be served on Saturday, March 9th at Noon. Please indicate in your registration form whether you would would like to be served lunch. Failure to do so may mean there is not enough food for everyone.

Please let us know in the "Dietary and Special Accommodations" section of the registration form if you require any special accommodation or have any dietery restrictions that must be considered for the lunch meal.

Grading

This is a grading up to 1-kyu. Registration and fees are due by February 24.

This is a CKF grading, as such must do the following:

  • Be a member the Canadian Kendo Federation
  • Have your annual CKF membership dues are up to date.
  • Be granted permission to grade by your sensei.
  • Register for the grading via the CKF Member Website

Dinner

The Saturday dinner is not included in the seminar fees. Details are TBD. Check back here later for updates!

Parking

The school has "Staff Parking" which is available for us to use for the weekend.

École Qayqayt Elementary is a non-smoking facility

Smoking, vaping, and tobacco in any form are strictly prohibited everywhere on the facility grounds.
