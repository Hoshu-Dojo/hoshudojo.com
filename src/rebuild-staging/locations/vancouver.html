Hoshu Dojo Vancouver is home to our lineage's first school in North America, started by Thomas Groendal, and has maintained a quiet but strong community presence for over ten years. Situated at the heart of Olympic Village, the second floor dance studio provides a serene view of the city while offering a central and accessible location for practice.

Registration and payment is managed by the community centre. Please pay at the front desk upon arrival, or via the Vancouver Parks Board online portal. You may be asked to present your receipt in class.

Please note that space is limited, and priority will be given to monthly registrants, though we will do our best to accommodate all in attendance.

A uniform and weapons are not required for the first few lessons. However, it is appreciated if you can contact us ahead of your first class so that spare weapons can be arranged.
