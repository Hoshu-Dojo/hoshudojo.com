Benefits

Tradition

The kata of Shindo Muso Ryu and Daito Ryu have been refined for centuries and are fascinating studies in the art of war. We take our duty to pass them on to future generations seriously.

Personal development

Koryu kata provide a structured place to grow resilient and confident in the face of conflict and find your voice under pressure.

Burn away stress and achieve a stronger, more flexible and coordinated body at the impact level you can handle.

Community

Our dojo family has gathered from all over North America and Japan to train with Goto sensei annually for over 10 years and values respect, authenticity, and humor.

Fun

It's fun. We love this stuff, and think you will too!

Arts

Hoshu Dojo is a community of students of Kei Goto sensei that are committed to carrying forward the cultural legacy of Shindo Muso Ryu Jojutsu and Daito Ryu Aikijujutsu that he has taught us. We each bring our authentic selves to practice and balance martial practice and moving zen with community and humor. We have locations in Japan, and satellite locations spanning the Pacific Northwest and other parts of the USA, Canada, and Mexico.

Shindo Muso Ryu Jojutsu, and its modern offshoot, Jodo, is a 400-year-old art that teaches the use of sword and broader martial and personal development through the use of a simple short staff to overcome a better-armed opponent wielding a sword. In Jodo, we rely on precision and the flexibility of the Jo to overcome the inherent advantages of our blade-wielding opponent.

Daito Ryu Aikijujutsu is the original “Aiki” art, an art of traditional Japanese jujutsu. This art includes sophisticated throws, joint locks, and strikes, offering a rich reserve of technical knowledge for overcoming an opponent in close range combat.
